Investment Overview

A Positioned Asset in Savannah's Most Active Corridor

This 6,542 SF single-story commercial building at 2812 Williams Street represents a compelling income-producing acquisition opportunity in the heart of Savannah's Victory Drive Corridor — one of the city's most rapidly evolving commercial and residential growth zones.

The property is offered with a Modified NNN lease structure at $23.00/SF annually, generating $150,466 in gross potential rent. Under this structure, the tenant assumes responsibility for all operating expenses — property taxes, insurance, utilities, and maintenance — with the landlord retaining only Roof and Structure obligations. This creates an exceptionally clean, low-management income stream.

The building sits on 1.04 acres with 28+ parking spaces across three lots, dual-street frontage, and a drive-in loading door — features that command premium rents and attract quality tenants. The property is immediately adjacent to national retailers including Whole Foods, ALDI, Target, and Home Depot, providing exceptional co-tenancy and foot traffic dynamics.

At $2,300,000 ($351.57/SF), this offering delivers a 6.20% stabilized cap rate — a strong risk-adjusted return in a high-growth coastal Georgia submarket with limited comparable supply.
