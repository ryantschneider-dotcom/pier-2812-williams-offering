Property Description

A Strategic
Position in
Savannah's
Hottest Corridor

This single-story, 6,542 SF building sits at the intersection of Williams Street and Grenoble Street, steps from Victory Drive and the Truman Expressway — Savannah's most rapidly evolving commercial and residential corridor. The property was recently acquired by a local investor and is immediately available for lease.

Originally built as a medical office facility, the building features an intelligent layout with three expansive open areas, a reception zone, five restrooms, multiple private offices, a conference room, and a staff lounge. A Cummins diesel generator on a concrete pad ensures uninterrupted operations — a rare and valuable feature for any business requiring continuous power.

An overhead drive-in door and rear loading ramp with double doors add exceptional utility for showroom, service, or distribution tenants. With 28+ parking spaces across three lots and dual-street frontage, this property delivers the visibility, accessibility, and infrastructure that serious operators demand.
